Choose your Wallet

You have to sync with the blockchain to use Shibacoin. MultiShiba is a "light" wallet. It syncs with the blockchain by "skimming" through the blockchain, providing fast sync times. Shibacoin Core, on the other hand, is a "full" wallet. It syncs by downloading it, providing a solid-working Shibacoin wallet.

What is Shibacoin?

Shibacoin is a decentralized, peer-to-peer digital currency that enables you to easily send money online. Think of it as "the internet currency."

The fun and friendly internet currency?

Shibacoin sets itself apart from other digital currencies with an amazing, vibrant community made up of friendly folks just like you."

What's with Shibacoin and the dog?

"Shiba" is our fun, friendly mascot! The Shiba Inu is a Japanese breed of dog that was popularized as an online meme and represents Shibacoin.
